MIBs for LSI RAID Controllers

To download the most recent LSI RAID controller MIBs:

1.      Go to http://www.lsi.com/storage_home/products_home/internal_raid/megaraid_sas/value_line/megaraid_sas_9261-8i/index.html.

2.      Click the Support and Downloads tab.

3.      Scroll down to the Miscellaneous section, and click the appropriate link to download the most recent version of the MegaRAID Storage Manager (MSM) .zip file for your operating system. For example, for Windows, click the MegaRAID Storage Manager - Windows (MegaRAID Release x.x.x) link.
