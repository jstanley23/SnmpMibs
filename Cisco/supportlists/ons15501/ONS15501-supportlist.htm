Cisco ONS15501 MIB Support Lists


The below MIBs are supported by the Cisco ONS15501 in release 3.0 and later. SNMP version 1 MIBs are in the v1 directory and SNMP version 2 MIBs are in the v2 directory. Note that for most every MIB .my in the v2 directory, there exists a SNMP version 1 conversion of the MIB-V1SMI.my in the v1 directory. The exception is SNMPv2-TC.my. Thanks go to David T. Perkins for his package smicng which was used to produce most of the v1 conversions.

The following is a list of MIBs supoported by the Cisco ONS15501, as of Release 3.0

ENTITY-MIB.my

ONS15501-MIB.my

SNMPv2-MIB.my
