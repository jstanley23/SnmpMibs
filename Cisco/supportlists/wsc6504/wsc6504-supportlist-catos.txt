The lists below indicate which MIBs and SNMP traps are supported by the
Catalyst 6000 series of switches (WS-C6006, WS-C6009, WSC-6503, WS-C6503-E,
WS-C6504-E, WS-C6506, WS-C6506-E, WS-C6509, WS-C6509-E, WS-C6509-NEB,
WS-C6509-NEB-A, WS-C6513, OSR-7609, CISCO7603, CISCO7606, CISCO7609)
running Catalyst OS software.
Depending on the Catalyst OS Software version of the supervisor 
module software, different MIBs and traps are supported.
