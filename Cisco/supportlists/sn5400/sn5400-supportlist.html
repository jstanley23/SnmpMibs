Cisco SN 5400 Family MIB Support List

The following list indicates the SNMP MIBs that are supported by the Cisco SN 5400 Family. The MIBs listed below are supported in version 3.3 or greater of the SN 5400 firmware.

All these MIBs are SNMP version 2 and are located in the v2 directory, expect for MIB-II which is SNMP version 1 and is located in the v1 directory.

Standard MIBs

 

ENTITY-MIB                        RFC 2737

ETHERLIKE-MIB                     RFC 2665

IF-MIB                            RFC 2863

MAU-MIB                           RFC 2668

MIB-II                            RFC 1213

 

Cisco MIBs

 

CISCO-CDP-MIB
CISCO-ENTITY-EXT-MIB
CISCO-ENTITY-FRU-CONTROL-MIB
CISCO-ENTITY-SENSOR-MIB
CISCO-SYSLOG-MIB
