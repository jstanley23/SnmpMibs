MIBs and Traps supported by the Catalyst 6000 series of switches 
(WS-C6006, WS-C6009, WSC-6503, WS-C6503-E, WS-C6504-E, WS-C6506,
WS-C6506-E, WS-C6509, WS-C6509-E, WS-C6509-NEB, WS-C6509-NEB-A,
WS-C6509-V-E, WS-C6513, ME-C6524GS-8S, ME-C6524GT-8S, OSR-7609, 
CISCO7603, CISCO7604, CISCO7606, CISCO7609) running Cisco IOS Software.
Depending on the Cisco IOS software version running on the Supervisor,
different MIBs and traps are supported.
