LightStream 1010 Network Management MIB Support Lists


The following MIBs are supported by the LightStream 1010 in release 11.1 and later.SNMP version 1 MIBs are in the v1 directory and SNMP version 2 MIBs are in the v2 directory. Note that for most every MIB .my in the v2 directory, there exists a SNMP version 1 conversion of the MIB -V1SMI.my in the v1 directory. The exception is SNMPv2-TC.my. Thanks go to David T. Perkins for his package smicng which was used to produce most of the v1 conversions.
