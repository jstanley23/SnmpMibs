C8510-CSR MIB Support Lists

The below MIBs are supported by the C8510-CSR in release 1.0 and later. SNMP version 1 MIBs are in the v1 directory and SNMP version 2 MIBs are in the v2 directory. Note that for most every MIB .my in the v2 directory, there exists a SNMP version 1 conversion of the MIB -V1SMI.my in the v1 directory. The exception is SNMPv2-TC.my. Thanks go to David T. Perkins for his package smicng which was used to produce most of the v1 conversions.
