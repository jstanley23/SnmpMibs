MIBs supported by the CiscoPro FastHub 316T and 316C
----------------------------------

MIBs supported in version 1.12 of the FastHub Network Management Module:

        RFC1213-MIB             (MIB-II)
        RFC1215-MIB             (MIB-II traps)
        EtherLike-MIB           (RFC 1643)
        RFC1317-MIB             (RS232-Like device, RFC 1317)
        SNMP-REPEATER-MIB       (RFC 1516)
        RMON-MIB                (Remote Monitoring, RFC 1757, first 4 groups only)
        CISCO-SMI               (Cisco Structure and Identification of Management Information)
        CISCO-STACKMAKER-MIB    (Cisco StackMaker MIB)
        CISCO-CDP-MIB           (Cisco Discovery Protocol MIB)
        CISCO-FASTHUB-MIB       (Enterprise MIB for the FastHub product)
