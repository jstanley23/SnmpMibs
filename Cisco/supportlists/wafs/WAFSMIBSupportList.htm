Cisco WAFS 3.0 MIB Support List

The following lists indicate the MIBs and SNMP notifications supported by CiscoWAFS 3.0 software release.

All of these are  SNMP (SMIv2) MIBs and are located in the v2 directory. Agent Capabilities files are listed under their associated MIBs and contain  implementation details on the MIB.  Agent Capabilities files may contain multiple AGENT-CAPABILITIES macros. You must refer to the  AGENT-CAPABILITIES macro that applies to your Cisco WAFS software release.

Note: Recently added MIBs are listed without hyperlinks because they are not yet published on Cisco.com.

Last updated 06/21/2005.
