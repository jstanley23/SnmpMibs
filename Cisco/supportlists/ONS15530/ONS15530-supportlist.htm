Cisco ONS15530 MIB Support Lists


The below MIBs are supported by Cisco ONS15530 in release 12.1 and later. SNMP version 1 MIBs are in the
v1 directory and SNMP version 2 MIBs are in the v2 directory. Note that for almost every MIB .my in the v2 directory,
there exists a SNMP version 1 conversion of the MIB-V1SMI.my in the v1 directory. The exception is SNMPv2-TC.my.
Thanks go to David T. Perkins for his package smicng which was used to produce most of the v1 conversions.
