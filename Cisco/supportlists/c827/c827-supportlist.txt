The Twister c827 and c827-4v routers support the following MIBs: 

    1. Standard MIBs:

          ADSL-DMT-LINE-MIB
          ADSL-LINE-MIB
          ATM-MIB
          ENTITY-MIB
          IF-MIB
          IGMP-MIB
          INT-SERV-GUARANTEED-MIB
          INT-SERV-MIB
          IPMROUTE-MIB
          NOVELL-IPX-MIB
          NOVELL-RIPSAP-MIB
          PIM-MIB
          RFC1213-MIB
          RFC1381-MIB
          RFC1398-MIB (ETHERMIB)
          RSVP-MIB
          SNMPv2-MIB
          TCP-MIB 
          UDP-MIB	

          The following MIBs are ILMI MIBs and cannot be accessed by an 
          NMS via SNMP; these MIBs are accessed via the ILMI protocol:          

          ATM-FORUM-ADDR-REG
          ATM-FORUM-MIB
       
          The following voice-related MIB is supported by c827-4v only:

	  DIAL-CONTROL-MIB

    2. Cisco MIBs:

          CISCO-AAL5-MIB
          CISCO-ADSL-DMT-LINE-MIB
          CISCO-ATM-EXT-MIB
          CISCO-BULK-FILE-MIB
          CISCO-CAR-MIB
          CISCO-FLASH-MIB
          CISCO-IETF-ATM2-PVCTRAP-MIB
          CISCO-IMAGE-MIB
          CISCO-IP-STAT-MIB
          CISCO-IPMROUTE-MIB
          CISCO-MEMORY-POOL-MIB
          CISCO-PING-MIB
          CISCO-QUEUE-MIB
          CISCO-SNAPSHOT-MIB
          CISCO-STACKMAKER-MIB
          CISCO-SYSLOG-MIB
          CISCO-TCP-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-CHASSIS-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-CPU-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-INTERFACES-MIB 
          OLD-CISCO-IP-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-MEMORY-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-NOVELL-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-SYSTEM-MIB
          OLD-CISCO-TCP-MIB
          
          The following voice-related MIBs are supported by c827-4v only:

          CISCO-DIAL-CONTROL-MIB
          CISCO-VOICE-ANALOG-IF-MIB 
          CISCO-VOICE-DIAL-CONTROL-MIB
          CISCO-VOICE-IF-MIB
